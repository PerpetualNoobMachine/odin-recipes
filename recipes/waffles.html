Gluten Free Waffles

Best gluten free waffle recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 cups gluten-free flour
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp chia seed
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Wisk wet Ingredients into dry ingredients and mix thoroughly
  3. Working in batches, laddel batter into waffle iron. Cook until golden crisp, about 3 to 5 minutes
